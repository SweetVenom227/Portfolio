About Me

Hello, I am Mia Seagrave Stanton, an artist and graphic designer. This site has been built based off of my middle name, Seagrave, it has always been something im proud of, a strong, dramatic name with a history of being the name of a firedepartment. While my name gave me inspiration, so did many other things, such as games, people and mythical stories, as is shown in my artwork. I am exited to share my self-built site and the displayed artwork with you today, feel free to look through and explore, I hope you enjoy!
